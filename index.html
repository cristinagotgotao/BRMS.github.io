BARANGAY RECORDS MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The Barangay Records Management System (BRMS) is a computerized system designed to organize, store, retrieve, and manage information related to the activities and operations of a barangay (a local administrative division in the Philippines)

Issuance of Certificate

BRMS enables barangay officials to generate and issue various certificates, such as barangay clearances, residency certificates, and other documents required by residents.

Resident Information Management

The BRMS allows barangay officials to store and manage the personal information of residents, including their names, addresses, contact details, and other relevant data.

Household Profiles

The system can create and maintain profiles for each household in the barangay, including the number of family members, relationships, and other household-specific information.

Case Management

BRMS provides a platform for barangay officials to manage and track cases within the community such as disputes, complaints, or any legal matters that require attention.
